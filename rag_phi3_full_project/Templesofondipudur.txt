
TEMPLES OF ONDIPUDUR, COIMBATORE

Arulmigu Mariamman Temple Ondipudur
Mariamman is worshipped as goddess of rain and disease protection.
Festival: Panguni / Chithirai Thiruvila
Rituals: Fire Walking, Neem Kambam Ritual

Arulmigu Sengodamman Temple Ondipudur
Folk ancestral deity worshipped by local families.
Festival: Aadi Velli Pooja
Tradition: Annadhanam

Arulmigu Veeramasthi Amman Temple Ondipudur
Associated with bravery, sacrifice and protection.
Used for obstacle removal prayers.

Arulmigu Sowdeswari Amman Temple Ondipudur
Devanga community temple.
Festival: Maha Parameswari Festival
Sword Ritual: Kathi Haakuva
